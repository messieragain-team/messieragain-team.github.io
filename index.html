Introducing Starjection

More than a planetarium. A new paradigm.

Bring the cosmos home.

Starjection is an innovative planetarium that fits homes, workplaces, and schools. Starjection packs quality, immersiveness, and portability in the same product — an unprecedented feat in the century-old industry.

Meet the innovation. Full-dome.

Starjection is the first product to utilze spherical mirror projection. This enables what sounds too good to be true; 180°, full-dome coverage with a normal office projector.

Starjection for education.

Our team, along with some 1 billion children in the world, come from a generation deprived of starlight. The shadow of urbanization took away the dark skies, but it doesn't have to take away the awe and marvel of space we deserve.

More on light pollution & why we build +

That's why Messier Again, from its beginning, had education as its primary goal. We reach more than enthusiasts and consumers; with a strong content ecosystem and manageable costs, Starjection will transform how students learn outside the classroom.

Content explosion.

Starjection seeks to establish a first-ever content ecosystem. Even the world's largest planetaria screen the same half a dozen films for a decades. We take dome content production from declining to vibrant through a market platform.

Forging Starjection's journey

In the 1800s, the astronomer Charles Messier mapped dozens of nebulae, clusters and galaxies with his fragile telescope. A lot has changed since his time. The disappearance of dark skies is alarmingly dramatic, even more so for the children of today and tomorrow.

That's why the three teens of Messier Again embarked on this journey. Building a new planetarium, for a new age, for a new generation.
